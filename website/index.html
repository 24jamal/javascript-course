Hello

On a quiet afternoon, a stray thought wandered through my mind about how oddly connected everything is—like how a cup of tea cooling on a desk somehow feels linked to the distant hum of traffic outside. A half-finished notebook lay open, its pages filled with ideas that once felt urgent but now seemed like echoes of a different moment. Somewhere nearby, a bird chirped with surprising confidence, as if announcing something important to a world that wasn’t really listening. It made me think about how we move through our days collecting small fragments—conversations, sounds, unfinished plans—and somehow, without noticing, they shape the stories we tell ourselves later.
